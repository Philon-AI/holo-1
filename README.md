# Holo 1.0: A Generalist Model that Navigates Virtual Worlds and Completes Hour-Long Objectives

> A 31B vision-language-action model that plays commercial 3D open-world games in real time.

**Authors:** Philon
**Contact:** info@philon.ai
**Technical Report:** [technical-report.pdf](technical-report.pdf)

## Abstract

Holo 1.0 is an open-source generalist model capable of completing extended missions across 3D open-world video games. Holo 1.0 integrates observation, reasoning, and action into a single model that processes raw 640×360 frames at 12 Hz and outputs keyboard–mouse actions at 21 Hz, engaging explicit reasoning only when necessary. Trained on 81,386 hours of gameplay across three curriculum stages — pre-training, instruction following, and reasoning — Holo 1.0 completes the introductory prologue region of *The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt* (White Orchard) in 78 minutes — on par with experienced human players (72 minutes) and ahead of novice players (98 minutes) — and the same model generalizes to *Starfield*, *Elden Ring*, and *Minecraft*, completing 20+ minute objectives in *Minecraft* despite its first-person voxel rendering and procedurally generated worlds.
